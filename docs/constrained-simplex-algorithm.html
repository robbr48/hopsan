Constrained Simplex Algorithm

The constrained simplex algorithm, also known as the complex algorithm, is based upon the simplex method developed by (Spendley et al, 1962) and (Nelder & Mead, 1965). It was first presented by (Box, 1965) and later improved by (Guin, 1968).

To optimize a model with n tunable parameters, we use k≥n+1 points in an n-dimensional parameter space, typically k=2n. The most important settings are the reflection coefficient , the randomization factor and the forgetting factor . The following procedure is used:
